The Question

For 400 years, chess writers have argued that White's first move is a real advantage. Black has spent the same 400 years trying to disprove it. We tested both sides on 1,797,006 club-level games from Lichess (Elo 1000–1599).

We came back with two contradictory answers — both supported by the same data, both featuring six visualizations, both linking to the same numbers. Click each side and judge for yourself.

Side A

Color is Destiny

White wins more. Always. Across every Elo band, every time control, and the strongest opening families. The first-move advantage is real, persistent, and visible at every level.
